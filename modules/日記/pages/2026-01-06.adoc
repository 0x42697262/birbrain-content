== HFMD

Hand, Foot, and Mouth Disease.

Common illness, very contagious.
Infects children younger than 5 years old (but can still infect older ones ofc).
Incubation period is 3 to 7 days.
Commonly caused by these viruses:
Enterovirus 71 and Coxsackievirus A10, A6, and A16.

Signs and Symptoms:

* Fever
* Sore throat
* Painful mouth sores that blister
* Rash commonly found on the hands and feet

Treatment: No treatment, just hope it gets away after 7 to 10 days.
